<p><strong>The Spectroscopic Reflectometry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Spectral Ellipsometer</li><li>Imaging Ellipsometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectroscopic reflectometry market features two primary types: automatic spectral ellipsometers and imaging ellipsometers. Automatic spectral ellipsometers are advanced instruments that measure the change in polarization of light reflected from a sample surface, providing detailed information on thin film properties and optical characteristics. Imaging ellipsometers, on the other hand, combine ellipsometric measurements with imaging techniques, enabling spatial mapping of sample properties. Both technologies are essential for applications in semiconductor manufacturing, materials science, and nanotechnology, enhancing precision and efficiency in measurements.</p></p>
