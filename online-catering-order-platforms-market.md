<p><p>Online catering order platforms can be categorized into two main market types: restaurant-controlled and independent markets. Restaurant-controlled platforms are owned and operated by specific restaurants, allowing them to manage their own menus, pricing, and customer interactions. This enables direct brand engagement and consistency in service. In contrast, independent market platforms serve as third-party intermediaries, providing access to a wide range of caterers and restaurants. This approach allows customers to compare various options and facilitate broader choices while streamlining the ordering process.</p></p>
