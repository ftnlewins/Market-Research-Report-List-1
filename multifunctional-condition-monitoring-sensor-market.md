<p><p>The multifunctional condition monitoring sensor market is categorized into two main types: "Wireless Type" and "Wired Type." Wireless sensors utilize radio communication technologies to transmit data without physical connections, offering flexibility and ease of installation, particularly in hard-to-reach areas. In contrast, wired sensors rely on physical cables for data transmission, providing reliable connectivity and stable performance, especially in environments with potential signal interference. Both types serve critical roles in monitoring equipment health and ensuring operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
