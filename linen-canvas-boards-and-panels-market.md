<p><p>Linen canvas boards and panels are essential surfaces for artists, varying primarily by their priming methods. Oil primed boards, on the other hand, are coated with an oil-based primer, suited for oil paints, offering a smoother finish and richer texture. Other types may include unprimed canvases or specialty textures, catering to different artistic techniques and preferences.</p></p>
