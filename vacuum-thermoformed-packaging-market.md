<p><p>The vacuum thermoformed packaging market consists of various materials, each serving specific needs. HIPS (High Impact Polystyrene) is valued for its durability and ease of molding, making it ideal for food and consumer goods. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) offers good transparency and rigidity, suitable for protective packaging. PETG is popular for its excellent clarity and chemical resistance. RPET, a recycled version of PET, appeals to eco-conscious consumers. PP (Polypropylene) is lightweight and versatile, while HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for its strength and moisture barrier properties.</p></p>
