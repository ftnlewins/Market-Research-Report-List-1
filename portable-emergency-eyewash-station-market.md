<p><p>The Portable Emergency Eyewash Station market is divided into two primary types: Ordinary Type Eyewash Stations and Pressure Type Eyewash Stations. Ordinary Type Eyewash Stations rely on gravity-fed systems to deliver water when activated, suitable for locations with limited risks. In contrast, Pressure Type Eyewash Stations use pressurized water to provide an immediate and powerful flow for emergency decontamination, making them ideal for environments with higher hazards. Both types are essential for ensuring quick eye irrigation in emergencies.</p></p>
