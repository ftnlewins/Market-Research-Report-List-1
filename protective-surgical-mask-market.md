<p><p>The protective surgical mask market primarily comprises two main types: flat-fold and cup type masks. Flat-fold masks are designed for convenience, featuring a collapsible structure that makes them easy to store and transport. They provide a snug fit around the face when unfolded. Cup-type masks, on the other hand, maintain a rigid shape, offering a more structured fit with enhanced breathing space. Both types are utilized in medical settings to protect against airborne pathogens, ensuring safety for healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
