Electric systems focus on battery-powered units, offering environmentally friendly and quieter operation. Hybrid systems combine both ICE and electric technologies, enabling greater flexibility and efficiency, reducing emissions while maintaining reliable climate control, appealing to operators looking to balance performance with sustainability.</p></p>
