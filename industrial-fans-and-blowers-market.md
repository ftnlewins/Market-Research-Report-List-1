<p><p>The industrial fans and blowers market primarily comprises two types: industrial fans and industrial blowers. Industrial fans are designed to move large volumes of air at low pressure and are commonly used for ventilation, cooling, and exhaust applications. In contrast, industrial blowers generate higher pressure to move air or gases, making them suitable for conveying materials and processes requiring increased airflow. Both types play critical roles across various industries, including manufacturing, HVAC, and power generation, ensuring efficient air movement and temperature control.</p></p>
