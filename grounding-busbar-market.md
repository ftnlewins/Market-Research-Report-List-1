<p><ul><li>Segregated Bus Duct</li><li>Nonsegregated Bus Duct</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The grounding busbar market features two main types: segregated bus ducts and non-segregated bus ducts. Segregated bus ducts have distinct compartments for each phase, minimizing electromagnetic interference and enhancing safety, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, non-segregated bus ducts house multiple phases within a single enclosure, offering simpler, cost-effective solutions for less critical environments. Both types play essential roles in electrical distribution systems, ensuring efficient grounding and enhancing operational safety across various industrial sectors.</p></p>
