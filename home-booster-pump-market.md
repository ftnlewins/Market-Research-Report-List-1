<p><p>The home booster pump market comprises primarily two types: single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for lower pressure applications, featuring a simple mechanism that efficiently boosts water flow for smaller homes. Conversely, multi-stage pumps utilize multiple impellers to generate higher pressure, making them suitable for larger properties or applications requiring elevated water pressure. Both types cater to different homeowner needs, balancing efficiency and performance to enhance water distribution in residential settings.</p></p>
