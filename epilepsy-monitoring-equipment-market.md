<p><p>The epilepsy monitoring equipment market is primarily divided into wearable devices and conventional devices. Wearable devices are innovative gadgets that enable continuous tracking of seizures through sensors integrated into accessories like wristbands or smart clothing. They offer real-time data and improve patient mobility. In contrast, conventional devices typically include stationary EEG machines and video monitoring systems used in clinical settings for comprehensive assessment and diagnosis. Both types aim to enhance patient care and seizure management, though they differ in application and technology.</p></p>
