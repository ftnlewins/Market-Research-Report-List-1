<p><p>The wearable MEMS sensors market consists of various types that enhance health monitoring and navigation. Pressure sensors monitor environmental pressure, and ambient temperature sensors track climatic conditions. Heart rate monitoring sensors assess cardiovascular health, while blood oxygen sensors gauge oxygen levels in the bloodstream.
