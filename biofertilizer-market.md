<p><strong>The Biofertilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nitrogen-Fixing</li><li>Phosphate-Solubilizing</li><li>Potash-Mobilizing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biofertilizer market is categorized into several types based on the function of the microorganisms involved. Nitrogen-fixing biofertilizers enhance soil nitrogen levels by converting atmospheric nitrogen into usable forms for plants. Phosphate-solubilizing biofertilizers improve phosphorus availability to plants by converting insoluble minerals into soluble forms. Potash-mobilizing biofertilizers increase potassium accessibility, critical for plant growth. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses various specialized biofertilizers that support nutrient uptake and promote overall soil health, contributing to sustainable agriculture.</p></p>
