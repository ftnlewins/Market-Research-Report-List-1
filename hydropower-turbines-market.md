<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Hydropower Turbines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Turbine</li><li>Reaction Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydropower turbines market is primarily divided into two types: impulse turbines and reaction turbines. Impulse turbines, such as Pelton wheels, utilize high-velocity water jets to convert kinetic energy into mechanical energy, ideal for high-head applications. Reaction turbines, like Francis and Kaplan turbines, operate with water pressure, generating power as water flows through them, suitable for low to medium-head situations. Both types cater to different operational needs, impacting overall efficiency and installation preferences in hydropower projects.</p></p>
