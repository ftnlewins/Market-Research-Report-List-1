Decanter brewers prepare coffee directly into glass decanters, ideal for smaller settings. Airpot brewers dispense coffee from airtight containers, maintaining temperature without direct heat. Coffee urns are large-capacity brewers suitable for events, capable of serving multiple cups simultaneously, perfect for gatherings or commercial use.</p></p>
