<p><ul><li>Regular Seeds</li><li>Feminized Seeds</li><li>Autoflowering Seeds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cannabis seeds market encompasses three main types: regular seeds, feminized seeds, and autoflowering seeds. Regular seeds can produce both male and female plants, suitable for breeding but uncertain for production. Feminized seeds are engineered to grow only female plants, maximizing yield and potency for growers seeking flower production. Autoflowering seeds transition from vegetative to flowering stages automatically, allowing for quicker harvest cycles regardless of light conditions. Each type serves different grower needs and cultivation strategies.</p></p>
