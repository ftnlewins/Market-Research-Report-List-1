<p><p>The FPD etcher market is categorized into two main types: dry etchers and wet etchers. Dry etchers utilize gaseous chemicals to etch materials, offering high precision and control, making them ideal for intricate designs. In contrast, wet etchers employ liquid chemicals to remove layers from substrates, generally ensuring faster processing and uniformity across larger areas. Both methods are crucial in the manufacturing of flat panel displays, with each type catering to specific applications and production requirements in the industry.</p></p>
