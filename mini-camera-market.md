<p><p>The mini camera market includes specialized types such as Near-Infrared (NIR) cameras and Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) cameras. NIR cameras excel in low-light and surveillance applications, capturing images based on wavelengths in the near-infrared spectrum, making them ideal for nighttime monitoring. SWIR cameras, on the other hand, operate in a slightly longer wavelength range, enhancing imaging capabilities in various conditions, including fog and smoke. Both types are increasingly used in industrial, agricultural, and security applications due to their advanced imaging features.</p></p>
