<p><ul><li>Unshaped (monolithic refractories)</li><li>Shaped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire brick market is divided into two primary types: unshaped (monolithic) refractories and shaped refractories. Unshaped refractories consist of materials like castables and gunning mixes, which can be molded into any desired form, providing flexibility for various applications. Shaped refractories, on the other hand, include bricks, tiles, and pre-formed shapes, offering standardized dimensions and properties for easier installation in high-temperature environments. Both types serve crucial roles in industries such as metal, glass, and cement manufacturing.</p></p>
