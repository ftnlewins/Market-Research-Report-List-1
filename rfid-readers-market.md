<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF</li><li>UHF</li><li>HF</li><li>MW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID readers operate across various frequency bands, each serving distinct applications. Low Frequency (LF) readers, typically operating at 125-134 kHz, are ideal for animal tracking and access control due to their short range. High Frequency (HF) readers, operating at 13.56 MHz, are commonly used in ticketing and contactless payment systems. Ultra High Frequency (UHF) readers, ranging from 300 MHz to 3 GHz, facilitate long-range scanning in logistics and supply chain management. Lastly, Microwave (MW) readers utilize higher frequencies for specialized applications, primarily in advanced tracking solutions.</p></p>
