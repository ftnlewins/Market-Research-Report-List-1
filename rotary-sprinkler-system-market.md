<p><ul><li>Steam Type</li><li>Ram Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary sprinkler system market includes various types, primarily categorized into steam type and ram type systems. Steam type sprinklers utilize steam pressure to create a rotating spray pattern, effectively irrigating large areas with minimal water waste. In contrast, ram type sprinklers leverage hydraulic power, using water pressure to drive a piston that rotates the sprinkler head. Both systems are designed for efficient water distribution, making them suitable for agricultural, landscaping, and industrial applications.</p></p>
