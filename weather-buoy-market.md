<p><p>The weather buoy market encompasses two primary types: solar-powered and battery-powered buoys. In contrast, battery-powered buoys rely on rechargeable batteries, offering flexibility in deployment but requiring periodic maintenance to replace or recharge batteries. Both types serve crucial roles in marine and atmospheric data collection, with their selection influenced by factors like operational environment, duration of deployment, and data transmission needs.</p></p>
