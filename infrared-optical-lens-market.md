<p><strong>The Infrared Optical Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aspheric Lens</li><li>Spherical Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared optical lens market comprises two main types: aspheric and spherical lenses. Aspheric lenses feature a non-spherical shape that reduces optical aberrations, enhancing image quality and performance, crucial for applications like surveillance and thermal imaging. In contrast, spherical lenses have a consistent curvature, making them easier to manufacture, but they may introduce more distortion in certain applications. Each type serves distinct needs in the infrared spectrum, with aspheric lenses generally preferred for high precision optics.</p></p>
