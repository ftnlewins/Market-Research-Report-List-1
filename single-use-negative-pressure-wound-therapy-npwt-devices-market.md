<p><ul><li>Canister Free</li><li>With Canister</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-use Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) devices are categorized into two market types: canister-free and with canister. Canister-free devices utilize integrated systems that collect exudate internally, eliminating the need for a separate collection canister, simplifying disposal and reducing the risk of leakage. Conversely, devices with a canister involve a conventional setup where wound exudate is collected in an external canister, allowing for larger volumes but requiring regular monitoring and maintenance. Both types provide effective therapeutic options for wound management.</p></p>
