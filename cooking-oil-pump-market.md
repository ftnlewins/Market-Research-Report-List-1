<p><strong>The Cooking Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Impeller Pump</li><li>Rotary Vane Pump</li><li>Air Operated Diaphragm Pump</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooking oil pump market includes several types, each serving specific needs. Flexible impeller pumps are ideal for transferring viscous oils due to their gentle handling and low shear. Rotary vane pumps offer efficient, high-pressure transfer, making them suitable for various cooking oils. Air-operated diaphragm pumps are versatile, providing safe and reliable operation without electrical components, suitable for hazardous environments. Other types may include gear pumps and screw pumps, catering to diverse applications in the food processing industry.</p></p>
