<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semi-Refined Carrageenan（SRC） Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Iota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semi-Refined Carrageenan (SRC) market encompasses three primary types: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda carrageenan. Kappa carrageenan forms strong gels and is used primarily in dairy products and meat applications. Iota carrageenan produces softer, more elastic gels, making it suitable for desserts and dairy dishes. Lambda carrageenan, which does not gel but thickens liquids, finds use in sauces and beverages. Each type serves distinct functions in the food industry, enhancing texture, stability, and mouthfeel in various products.</p></p>
